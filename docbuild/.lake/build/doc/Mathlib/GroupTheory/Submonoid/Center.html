    Centers of monoids #

    Main definitions #

    • Submonoid.center: the center of a monoid
    • AddSubmonoid.center: the center of an additive monoid

    We provide Subgroup.center, AddSubgroup.center, Subsemiring.center, and Subring.center in other files.
