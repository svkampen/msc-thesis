    Infinite sums and products over ℕ and ℤ #

    This file contains lemmas about HasSum, Summable, tsum, HasProd, Multipliable, and tprod applied to the important special cases where the domain is ℕ or ℤ. For instance, we prove the formula ∑ i ∈ range k, f i + ∑' i, f (i + k) = ∑' i, f i, ∈ sum_add_tsum_nat_add, as well as several results relating sums and products on ℕ to sums and products on ℤ.
